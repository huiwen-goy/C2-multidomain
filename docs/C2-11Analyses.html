Comparison with Hamalainen et al.results

Similar findings:
* Worse BPTA, higher asymmetry, self-report more trouble hearing in noise ~ more hearing difficulty
* Better memory(?), driving/good transport, higher social network index ~ more hearing difficulty

They found these effects, but we did not:
* Male sex, device use, higher multimorbidity, (life space index)
